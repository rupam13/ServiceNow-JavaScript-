## 🎯 Why This Approach Works

### 1. **Simple Language**
❌ Bad: "Instantiate a variable and initialize it with a string value"  
✅ Good: "Make a box and put your name in it"

### 2. **Tons of Practice**
❌ Bad: 2-3 exercises, then move on  
✅ Good: 25+ exercises, you really understand it

### 3. **Baby Steps**
❌ Bad: Learn 10 concepts in one day  
✅ Good: Learn 1 concept, practice it 25 times

### 4. **Quick Learning**
❌ Bad: Spend 20 minutes on one concept  
✅ Good: 5 minutes per concept, then practice

### 5. **Real Examples**
❌ Bad: "Print 'hello world'"  
✅ Good: "Tell the computer your name and age"
